Ethan Chey is a student at Charles W. Flanagan High School in Pembroke Pines, Florida. He spends his time making apps and learning programming languages. His research interests include computational linguistics, computer science, engineering, and more.

He is an editor on the school yearbook, the secretary and social media manager for the Falcon Sound, the school's band; a member of Political Activism and Voter Engagement club, a member of the school's Game Club, and a member of the Science National Honor Society.

He has taken several Advanced Placement and AICE courses, as well as earned numerous industry certifications, including Office 2016, Intro to Programming, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Dreamweaver, and more.

In his free time he plays games and makes YouTube videos, as well as play music.
